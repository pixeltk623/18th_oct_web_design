Early life

Sharma was born on 30 April 1987 in Bansod, Nagpur, Maharashtra. His mother, Purnima Sharma, is from ,Visakhapatnam.His father, Gurunath Sharma, worked as a caretaker of a transport firm storehouse. Sharma was raised by his grandparents and uncles in Borivali because of his father's low income. He would visit his parents, who lived in a single-room house in Dombivli, only during weekends.[3] He has a younger brother, Vishal Sharma

Youth and domestic first-class career

Sharma made his List A debut for West Zonea against Central Zone in the Deodhar Trophy at Gwalior in March 2005. Batting at number eight, he scored 31 not out as West Zone won by 3 wickets with 24 balls remaining. Cheteshwar Pujara and Ravindra Jadeja made their debuts in the same match.It was Sharma's unbeaten innings of 142 in 123 balls against North Zone at the Maharanna Bhupal College Ground in Udaipur in the same tournament that brought him into the limelight.[7] He visited Abu Dhabi and Australia with the India A squad and was then included among India's 30-member probables list for the upcoming ICC Champions Trophy tournament, although he did not make the final squad

International career

Further information: List of international cricket centuries by Rohit Sharma
As of 4 September 2021, Sharma had played in 381 international matches (43 Test, 227 ODI, 111 T20I) and scored 41 centuries (8 Test, 29 ODI, 4 T20I)

Achievements

See also: List of highest individual scores in One Day International cricket
Sharma holds the world record for the highest individual score by a batsman playing in a one-day international match, having scored 264 against Sri Lanka at Eden Gardensa, Kolkata, on 13 November 2014. He is the only player to have scored three double-centuries in this form of international cricket.[83] In January 2020, Sharma was named as the ODI Player of the Year by the International Cricket Council (ICC). During the 2019 World Cup, Sharma became is the only batsman to have scored Five hundreds in a single edition in World Cup history.
